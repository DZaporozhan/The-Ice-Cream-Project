how it’s made?

The taste and quality of milk depend on the diet of cows. We grow our own food and create a balanced menu based on it for every inhabitant of our farm.

At one time, the breed was bred by selecting parents with excellent health and high milk yields. As a result of many years of work, the best dairy breed was obtained, which can be safely called the cream of cow society.

All cows on our farm belong to the Holstein-Friesian breed.
